Bio

I’m Tamsen Brooke Warner, is a passionate professional native to New England with more than 10 years of experience in administration, small nonprofits, and bookkeeping. She graduated from the University of Connecticut and was the first person in her family to earn a baccalaureate degree. Now in New Brunswick, she is studying Information Technology + Network Administration. In her spare time, Tamsen is an avid reader, cat addict, coloring book enthusiast, and fledgling ceramicist. Currently, she makes pottery at Sunbury Shores Arts + Nature Centre and previously worked out of Watershed Center for the Ceramic Arts, as well as Ticonic Gallery + Studios. In the theatre industry, Tamsen has had the pleasure to work as a scenic painter, stage manager (and more!) with Waterville Opera House, Ogunquit Playhouse, Connecticut Repertory Theatre, and The Muny.
